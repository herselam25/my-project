Teddy-Afro

Tewodros Kassahun (Amharic: ቴዎድሮስ ካሳሁን; born July 14, 1976 in Addis Ababa), known professionally as Teddy Afro, is an Ethiopian singer-songwriter. Teddy Afro made his debut in 2001 with the album Abugida, quickly establishing himself as a prominent voice in his native country. Mixing reggae, traditional Ethiopian styles, and pop music. His second album Lebo was done in 2000 but released in 2002.
